INFINITE MARIO balancing test - Revision 3

Below are links that lead to batches of Infinite Mario featuring different boredom/stress levels (game balancing).

Each batch has 3 games. You can jump to the next game of any batch by clicking the green CONCLUDE button at the bottom of the game page.

  • Batch 1: games A1 (easy, not necessarily boring), A2 (a bit more challenging than A1) and A3 (a bit more challenging than A1 and A2, expected to be somewhat stressful).
  • Batch 2: games B1 (auto-scrolling and extremelly easy, designed to be boring), B2 (more challenging than A2) and B3 (a bit more challenging than A3, expected to be somewhat stressful).
  • Batch 3: all games from A to B. This batch gives a better contextualized way of measuring the increase of boredom/stress throughout the games.

P.S. You DO NOT have to answer the questionnaires, just click SUBMIT.
